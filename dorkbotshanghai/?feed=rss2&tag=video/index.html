Xindanwei is hosting a workshop on vvvv on Saturday 29 May from 2pm. vvvv is a toolkit for real-time video synthesis. It is designed to facilitate the handling of large media environments with physical interfaces, real-time motion graphics, audio and video that can interact with many users simultaneously.

This is a workshop that should interest some dorkbot regulars and is a good opportunity to get hands-on experience with vvvv.
