孟奇｜Qi Meng – 《孟奇的自制声音产品 | Homemade products for sound and music》 孟奇设计并且制作了一些用于声音与音乐制作的设备。这其中包括六振荡器，双环形调制器和为易学和易用性优化过键位的MIDI键盘等。

Meng Qi had designed and made several pieces of gears for sound and music creation. Including 6 oscillators, dual diode ring modulator, midi keyboard with special note layout for easy learning/playing.

孟奇，85年生于北京，13岁开始弹吉他，15岁开始用电脑玩音乐。慢慢的接触合成器，采样器，效果器，等等。05年签了ChinaVibe Records，05年底发《山水恋》，06年底发《山水·颤》。09年ChinaVibe Records解散。从05年开始做Live P.A。直到09年，对于舞曲现场演出失去了兴趣。从08年加入痴人乐队至今。在摸索音乐的日子里，因为对设备的钟爱和想要寻找对自己来讲最富表现力乐器 的诉求，开始了自己的DIY之路。他还在Midifan杂志上分别发表了模拟合成器制作以及音乐制作的教程 (http://www.midifan.com)。最新专辑将于12年初发表。

Qi Meng, Born in 85, Beijing, began playing guitar on 13 yr old, started making music with computer on 15. Signed ChinaVibe Records in 2005, 2 CD albums released in CVR: 2005 – Landscape In Love, 2006 – Landscape On Live. Now is the band member of Beijing local rock band “Chiren” since 2008. (http://space.menllo.com/chiren), working on synthesizing, sampling, keyboard and some other instruments. Because of the love for gears and the will to find the best instrument for myself, I’ve also been doing instrument DIY. His analog syntheszing tutorials and music production tutorials were released in Midifan Magazine (http://www.midifan.com).New album is in post production and will be released early 2012.http://www.mengqimusic.com/

Kim Laughton | Olivepixel -《 如何让模拟与数字互相碰撞结合以产生奇幻的视觉效果 – Mixing analogue and digital video to achieve otherwise unobtainable effects
介绍模拟与数字视频混合在一起后会有怎样的优势和缺点，以及通过这种方式，会达到的一些怎样特别的视觉效果。这种手段不仅可以在现场视觉工作种使用，同时也可以作为传统视频处理种的一部分。

The advantages and disadvantages of mixing analogue and digital video, and the unique effects that can be created. This will be applicable for both live video work or as part or more conventional video production.

About Kim Laughton

Kim在上海已经做了两年多的VJ，大部分的工作都是与ROM (www.romshanghai.com)相关，同时，他也活跃在中国各地，在许多现场表演，音乐节以及一些合作项目中你都能看到他的身影。

I’ve been VJing in Shanghai for over two years  and spend most of my time working with ROM (www.romshanghai.com) I’ve played at various venues around China including live shows, music festivals and corporate events.

www.olivepixel.com

张翊｜Yi Zhang －《Milkymist One－开源音乐视觉化硬件》Milkymist One 是一款开源硬件，它不用接电脑，可以即开即用的视频合成器，它可以将现场输入的音频和视频转换成奇妙的视觉艺术效果，通过投影仪或显示屏展现出来。它可以 用在音乐会，演出，俱乐部，艺术节，会展，和聚会中。简单而又丰富的 Flickernoise Patching (FNP) 语言，让任何一个人都能创造出独特而具个性化的视觉艺术效果，即便你不懂任何计算机语言也没有关系。

Milkymist One is an open-source hardware, a video synthesizer to create real-time visual effects from audio or video input. It can be used as a turnkey solution without any modification. No computer needed. But it doesn’t stop there.The simple yet rich Flickernoise Patching (FNP) language allows any individual to create unique and personal visual effects without requiring any computer programming experience.

张翊，继获得美国阿拉斯加大学计算机软件工程专业硕士后，在美国的软件公司DataViz, Inc. 做了十年的软件工程师和团队领头人，她参与和领头开发编程的智能手机办公室文档处理软件DocumentsToGo被上百万的iPhone, Blackberry, Nokia, Motorola, Sony Ericsson, 和Treo的手机用户使用着。在2008年，抱着对自由软件和开源代码理念的热忱，张翊加入了台湾的Openmoko公司，这家公司制造了全球第一台开源 智能手机Neo 1973 和 FreeRunner。到2009年，张翊和多年的同事又将这种对开源的热忱更提升了一步，一起创办了他们自己的开源硬件公司 Sharism at Work Ltd.，迄今为止，他们开发制造了两个开源产品，Milkymist One就是2011年底出来的最新产品。

After gained her Master Degree of Computer Science – Software Engineering from University of Alaska USA, Yi Zhang worked as a software engineer and team lead at DataViz, Inc., an independent software company in the USA. For over 10 years, she developed office suite software DocumentsToGo, which are being used by millions of users on iPhone, Blackberry, Nokia, Motorola, Sony Ericsson, and Treo smartphones. In 2008, driven by the passion for free software and open source, Yi joined Openmoko Inc., a company in Taiwan, who made the first open source smartphone Neo 1973 and FreeRunner. In 2009, Yi took a step even further to co-found her own open source hardware company Sharism at Work Ltd.. The company now has produced 2 open source products. Milkymist One is the new one, which just came out November 2011.

郭锐文｜Raven Kowk – 《 影像的地貌景观重构 | Lanscape》一 个 影像向三维地貌景 观单向转化的重构过程实验。在这个实验中,我选取了平淡无奇的生活照、影片,戒是安装 于一个人流密集点的摄像头捕捉的实时影像这些本身幵不具有特别的美感且让人习以为常 的影像素材。通过自己编写的 Processing 程序,将这些影像素材转化成为一张宏观的地貌 景观图,从而重置观者对各种影像的惯有体验。

An experiment on the reconstruction process of image transforming into landscape in 3D. In the experiment, I picked some ordinary static or moving images as source materials. These materials can be photos, video clips or real-time image captured by a camera which is set up in a populous area. Through the transition program which I created using Processing, a great landscape will be generated, and it will reset the normal experience of a viewer.

郭锐文，1989 年生于上海。2001 年在当时盛行的闪客文化影响下开始接触 Flash,2003 年开始自学 ActionScript 脚本。2007 年至 2011 年间就读于上海视觉艺术学院,摄影专业。2010 年开始 自学 Processing。2011 年加入机器人设计研发团队 RoboPeak,同年 10 月在上海新车间 Hackerspace 长期开设 Processing 工作坊。

Raven Kwok was born in Shanghai in 1989. He started to create Flash works in 2001 under the influence of Chinese “Flasher” Wave and took up self-study in ActionScript in 2003. He studied in Shanghai Institute of Visual Art from 2007 to 2011, majoring in photography.  In 2010, Raven’s focus began to shift from Flash to Processing. In 2011, he joined RoboPeak, a  research  and development team in robotics platforms and applications. In October the same year, he initiated his Processing Workshop at Xinchejian Hackerspace in Shanghai
———————————-

Patrick Jost – 《我辞职后的生活——与互动设计项目有关的工作与生活》 我会介绍我是如何成为自由互动设计师的经过，以及我是如何接到这些互动项目的。现场会对我的个人作品做一些展示，比如一个是体育街机游戏（www.sportpong.com）， 另一个是一个装置作品Ghostframe（www.fuenfstern.com）；还有一些商业作品（Vertu, BMW, EAton, 上海世博会奥地利馆, 亚特兰蒂斯酒店儿童角, Farnell ）。最后会对与vvvv这个软件以及vvvv社群做一个简短的介绍。

How i started to become a freelancer doing interaction design projects ( i was an industrial printer before working by contract)how i work and how the jobs get to me. presentation of some of my personal projects (www.sportpong.com a sporty arcade game, and Ghostframe, an exhibition installation i did for www.fuenfstern.com)  and some commercial work (Vertu, BMW, Eaton, World Expo Shanghai for austrian pavillion, Atlantis Hotel Kids Corner, Farnell). Quick introduction to the vvvv software and the vvvv community.

About Partick Jost

Partick Jost (elektromeier)1975年生于瑞士，和许多同龄的孩子一样自幼使用commodore amiga电脑进行有趣的图形创作。1997-1999年期间接受过基础的艺术与设计教育. 在2003年的时间为了vjing的目的从而接触到vvvv,在此之前已接触过类似的图形编程软件，例如puredata和reaktor。从此后与 vvvv一见钟情。从2008年之后Partick jost决定成为一名自由职业者，通过使用vvvv曾经为多家公司做过许多互动多媒体展示项目。

Partick Jost (elektromeier) born 1975 in switzerland. like many boys at his age he became addicted to computers early, playing around on the commodore amiga, and developed an interest for computer graphics.

He did a basic art&design education 1997-1999 in the cities of St. Gallen and Basel Switzerland. He did an education and worked as an industrial printer from 2000-2004.

To vvvv he came in 2003 for vjing purposes. At that time he already knew graphical patching applications for music generations like pd and reaktor, and he was very exited that something like this existed for computer graphics.

Since 2008 he decided to work fully as a freelancer and make his living just with vvvv patching.

Francis Lam

Wei Wei

Tobias Rosenberger

Then none others than *LLND wrapped up the afternoon with a talk on circuit bending and an audio-visual performance that left the audience truly inspired. A very fitting end to the third gathering of the dorkbot community in Shanghai.

Speaker profiles:

//=s=p=e=a=k=e=r=//

*LLND is Laurent Lettree and Nathalie Delpech, intermedia artists from Paris. Their work focuses on interactive projects they call instrumental sculptures, and audio-visual performances. For visuals *LLND use a setup of rotating tables, cameras, multiple objects, and a multichannel video mixer – making it possible to do real-time cinema and special effects without using a computer. For sound *LLND use Max/MSP to link images and sounds, various sensors, physical objects and real-time processing together with Ableton Live.

For dorkbot Shanghai #3 *LLND will bring some of their live material and perform extracts as well as discussing associated themes and the techniques used.
More info: http://www.llnd.fr + http://www.myspace.com/kinoloops + http://www.myspace.com/pechou

//=s=p=e=a=k=e=r=//
Francis Lam received his master’s degree from the MIT Media Laboratory in 2006. He is an expert in the social aspects of digital media and how people communicate in computer-mediated environments. Mobile is another field to which he is dedicated. Back in 2004, Francis and his team developed an emerging application – Versatile Messaging System ­ that allows users to send customized video and animated messages. In addition, Francis is also a talented designer and media artist. He is particularly interested in creating new interfaces and applications for online communication. His award-winning project “db-db.com” created a new web arena for exhibiting online identities and connecting like-minded individuals.

For dorkbot Shanghai #3, Francis will present his recent work on an infrared tracking project and particle systems visualization for Nike, and talk about his creative approach to mobile applications development.
More info: http://db-db.com/

//=s=p=e=a=k=e=r=//
Wei Wei is a young multimedia artist and designer with a great passion for discovering interesting things. His works focuses on interactive media designing and experimental multimedia researching, which uses visual arts, science, new media, and perception as means of studies to create cross-disciplinary works and solve complex problems. Wei Wei holds a B.A. degree in Experimental  Multimedia Design from China Central Academy of Fine Arts (CAFA).

For dorkbot Shanghai #3, Wei Wei will present some of his work with a special focus on his Mood Machine installation, an interactive installation using Tesla coil.
More info: http://www.vimeo.com/6787489

//=s=p=e=a=k=e=r=//
Tobias Rosenberger studied “Applied Theatre Studies” in Giessen / Germany and works at the crossroads of theatre, new media art and installation. Besides various performances (Festival Junger Talente, Frankfurter Positionen, Körber Studio Junge Regie) he realized i.a. installations for the German + French Embassy in Sana’a / Yemen, the Goethe Institut in Barcelona and Franken Architekten. He was three times participant of Luminale Frankfurt (2006, 2008, 2010) and exhibited widely, including Lyon (Les Subsistences), Copenhagen (re-new-DigitalArtsFestival), Liverpool (Moves10), Katowice (Galeria Rondo Sztuki), Mexico-City (solo in Galería A/B del CENART), Osnabrück (European Media Art Festival, Kunsthalle Dominikanerkirche), and Esslingen/Neckar (solo in Villa Merkel). Rosenberger has been awarded grants from Goethe Institut and Werkleitz (1. Mexico-scholarship for German New Media artists, Centro Multimedia del Centro Nacional de las Artes), the City of Esslingen (Esslinger Bahnwärter Visual Arts) and Foundation Künstlerdorf Schöppingen. He has held residencies at European Mobile Lab for Interactive Media Art (e- MobiLArt, 2008/2009) and at Pépinières européennes pour jeunes artistes, map-extension 2009- 2010 programme (Festival Okuparte, Huesca/Spain). Currently he is based in Beijing.

For dorkbot Shanghai #3, Tobias will present three of his more recent works; one about hardware-hacking (The An-Archic Device or Cold Lights), another one about hacking of surveillance cameras (Large Scale or Breathing City) and finally one work that is based on the military chapters of ancient Chinese philosopher and engineer Mozi and makes use of 3d-prints and other technological stuff (The Grand Defender).
More info: http://www.tobiasrosenberger.de/

About dorkbot Shanghai:
With electricity connecting the dots; artists, hackers, engineers, inventors, DIY enthusiasts, and people just like you, regularly meet at dorkbot events in over 85 cities around the world to exchange ideas in casual surroundings. The trusted motto of dorkbot worldwide is “people doing strange things with electricity.” dorkbot Shanghai is simply the Shanghai chapter of dorkbot.
More info: http://dorkbot.org/dorkbotshanghai

About the presenters:
Super Nature is a Shanghai based multi-discipline design company. It specialises in interactive design, visual communication & media technology. It takes on challenges through experimentations in new media and physical computing.
More info: http://www.supernaturedesign.com

ARTLINKART is an online database project for Chinese contemporary art – a bilingual art website for both Chinese and English readers.
More info: http://www.artlinkart.com

Ben Houge has been active in the Chinese experimental music/sound art scene since 2004, having performed at most of China’s major festivals and venues. He has presented his real-time, algorithmic sound and/or video installations at Beijing’s Today Art Museum, Shanghai’s OV Gallery, and Art+Shanghai Gallery. Ben holds a Master’s degree in classical composition from the University of Washington, but has also been a pop songwriter since grade school, and the main subject of his art is to find connections between all of these disparate threads, disciplines, and practices.
More info: http://benhouge.com/

Justin Sebastian studied jazz-trumpet at Folkwanghochschule in Essen. Between 2000 and 2003 he gained experience in Thai traditional music in
Bangkok. Throughout his career he has worked independently with artists such as Nusara Mai-ngarm, Peter Kowald, Geraldo Si, DJ Orson, Sebastian Gramss, Christoph Iacono, Adam Holzman, Jean Sasportes. Currently Justin Sebastian is a member of the “WIO” (Wuppertal Improvisers Orchestra) and is active in smaller formations and as a soloist in Germany.
