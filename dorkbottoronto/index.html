12 Marc ‘09

Marcia Huyer, Paulette Phillips

 

 

 

Lonely Architectures

 

Both Huyer’s and Phillips’ work evoke quiet, contemplative and

beautifully weird moments. Huyer works with eerily lit, inflatable

 tyvek architectural outgrowths. Phillips constructs eerie

installations  that probe latent meanings – the memory

of her Floating House video haunts me still, years after seeing it.
