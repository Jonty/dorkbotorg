dorkbot pittsburgh's Spring 2013 meetings!

We took a time-out in the Fall of 2012, and now that we've rested up we're lining up speakers for the Spring 2013 meetings.

If there's someone you'd like to see give a presentation or a topic you'd like to learn about, drop us email at dorkbotpgh at dorkbot.org.

About dorkbot pittsburgh

dorkbot pittsburgh is open to the public, non-smoking, all-ages, and admission is free. We meet upstairs at Brillobox, vegetarian dinner and alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks are available.

If you'd like to speak at dorkbot, if you know someone you'd like to see speak, or if there's a topic you'd like to learn more about, please let us know.

Brillobox is donating space and resources for the meeting, so consider staying after for drink or showing up before for an early dinner to thank them for their support. Brillobox is a non-smoking bar and dorkbot pittsburgh is proud to be a non-smoking event.

Getting to Brillobox

Brillobox is located at 4104 Penn Ave (at Main) in the Bloomfield neighborhood of Pittsburgh, PA.

Mailing Lists, RSS Feeds, and twitter
The dorkbot pittsburgh mailing lists are:

  • dorkbotpgh-announce: moderated, announcements only, no chatter
  • dorkbotpgh-blabber: unmoderated, open discussion for dorkbot pittsburgh and related topics

We're on twitter as dorkbotpgh, posting pointers to interesting things related to pittsburgh and the dorkbot mindset.

Subscribe to the RSS feed for news and event listings or the podcast feed to see video of any meeting you might have missed.
