Getting Involved

If you'd like to present your work or show off some technology at an upcoming dorkbot, drop us a line or find one of us before or after a dorkbot event.

Please remember that dorkbot is all volunteer and all non-profit. Nobody gets paid, no money changes hands nor are we a free advertising forum for businesses.
