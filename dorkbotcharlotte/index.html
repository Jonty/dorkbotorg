Dorkbot Charlotte is currently organizing it's first event. We are looking for presenters, a venue, and people to come mingle. And anything you you'ld like to contribute.

Dorkbot events can take several forms, from having two or three 30 minute presentations, to quick lightning round presentations by many people.

Join our mailing lists to keep track of events, or to help plan one yourself. Join the announcements only list for announcements from the dorkbot organizers, and/or join the discussion list for dorkbot discussions.

The main dorkbot site is http://dorkbot.org/.
Dorkbot Charlotte was kick-started by Matt Gilbert and JT Helms on 7 June, 2005.
