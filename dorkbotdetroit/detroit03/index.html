Dorkbot.detroit 03
10 June 2005

Strings and Things

The third Dorkbot.detroit focused on experimental audio made with strings and other things. Paticapants brought with an assortment of homemade and modified stringed instruments creating an improvised ambient atmosphere. The started with a performance form Steve Curtin that was followed by Bubblegone and Verzerren. Afterwards everyone got together for collabritive performance.

Apologies for there being no photos of this session. There were some difficulties with the transfer and all were lost.

Steven Curtin
A hybrid musician/engineer. He has been building and playing
electronic instruments and guitars for over twenty years, including work for such
companies as Passport and Ensoniq.

Steven contributed some guitar-originated sound sculpture using electric sarod/autoharp, just intonation fret guitar, e-bow, doorhinge slide, digital delay/sampler pedal, harmonizer and custom-programmed DSP multitap delay. For more information see http://curtin.emf.org/.

verzerren vs. bubblegone
The duo performed an improvised set that involved bubblegone using an antique toy xylophone and acoustic guitar for sound sources. This led to on the fly sampling and effecting by both vezerren and him.
