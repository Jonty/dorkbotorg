Signal Processing Chain Phase II
3 March 2006 - 7:30pm, Dreamland Theater, Ypsilanti, MI

Our second signal processing chain saw a lot of new faces as well as a lot new ideas on how the chain was constructed.

Once again there were seven particpants with an eclectic collection of manipulation devices. We experimented with creating loops within the chain and splitting the audio signal to two different chains of three people with the final sound going to two different PAs. The audio sources for this chain were an old keytec K30 keyboard and the wonderful guitar playing of Tom Barton.
