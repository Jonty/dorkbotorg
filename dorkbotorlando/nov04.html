NOV 2004

We finally had our second meeting after being pummeled by four hurricanes, and it was a great success! Mac Rutan Kicked off the show by presenting concepts from a piece entitled "Divided We Speak", an interactive experience which allowed the triggering of audio samples based on ultra sound positioning. Dave Dennison, Rob Pope (aka vj ADD), and Geneva broke out their VJ gear and presented some amazing real-time control over various types of video footage, including a system for scratching video using existing turntables presented by Justin Kent.
