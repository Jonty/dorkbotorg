Friday, September 15, 2006

 

Dorkbot 1.02!



Our second installment of Dorkbot (after a long summer hiatus) focused
primarily on video and open source. Documentation of Carrie Gates presentation
on video djing can be found at www.archive.org


Ian Campbell talked about the linux live cd dynebolic which will take over your computer, non-permanently install a raft of interesting open source graphics and sound manipulating utilities and let you experience linux very effortlessly. You can download it from their main site www.dynebolic.org.

Also of note is the site distrowatch where you can find ratings and reviews of other linux distributions. A good place to start your exploration of open source operating systems.

Thursday, June 29, 2006

 

Saskatoon plays with Electricity!

The inaugural Dorkbot meeting was held Saturday June 17th @ 1pm.
Tim Dallet talked about works from the subtle technologies
conference and Ian Campbell gave a brief discussion and demonstration
of verious types of microcontrollers and their uses. We handed out a questionaire
regarding the continuing emphasis of dorkbot and encouraged participants to suggest
topics and speakers for the future.
