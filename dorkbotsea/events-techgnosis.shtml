Come celebrate the opening night of the art exhibit "People Doing Strange Things with Electricity II" and the CD launch of "People Doing Strange Things with Electricity Too"

8pm to midnight | all ages | bar 21+
opening exhibit at CoCA featuring music from DJMB and Carlos Miguel, including tracks from the People Doing Strange Things With Electricity Too CD, and experimental musician Ffej.
For visual artists see our PDSTWEII page
For Music artists see our PDSTWEToo page

9pm to 2am | 21+ | 2 full bars
After party six short blocks east at Consolidated Works featuring DJ Venus (deep sexxy & disco house), novaTRON vs. AtoMikDoG (live P.A., from break beatz to deep house), DJML (funky, progressive house).

Experience visual art and installations including VJ FoxFireFX, VJ Scobot, Ben Bogart (Live Visual Performance), Light Sound Glass: multimedia sculputure by Prograta (Joe Gray and Gabe Henderson), Rattlesnake Dick's Headlights, HypKnowTron by Gaspo and more...

5$ suggested donation
Wear electricity! Bring a robotic date!

 
   
 

The title of this opening event comes from Erik Davis’s acclaimed book
‘TechGnosis: myth, magic + mysticism in the age of information’.
Find out more about Erik, and TechGnosis, at http://www.techgnosis.com.
