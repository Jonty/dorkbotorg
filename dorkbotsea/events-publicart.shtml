CELEBRATE AND DEBATE PUBLIC ART!

Artist Julia Cole, CoCA and dorkbotsea present…

A DISCUSSION OF PUBLIC ART IN SEATTLE
A CELEBRATION OF THE SPOKEN WORD

and

THE LAUNCH OF THE POETRY MACHINE!!!

On FRIDAY, JUNE 10th FROM 6 PM TO LATE at CoCA
All Ages
Admission is FREE (suggested $5 donation)

BE THE FIRST TO step into former Seattle resident, now Chair of Interdisciplinary Studies
at Kansas City Art Institute, Julia Cole’s latest mobile, interactive public art installation –

THE POETRY MACHINE!

Try out the artwork before it embarks on its week-long tour of public venues in and
around Seattle, from SeaTac International Airport to Seattle Center! Reconstruct
couplets of famous poets with words from daily life, or improvise your own poetry
through the movements of your body inside the booth!

ALSO

Join a lively panel discussion on public art in this region! Moderated by writer and
activist JOHN BOYLAN, hear public artists with 100 YEARS OF COMBINED EXPERIENCE
in both creating and commissioning works of art in public places and spaces in Seattle,
the West Coast and beyond discuss the strengths, weaknesses, opportunities and
challenges facing artists and administrators in this field. YOUR COMMENTS AND QUESTIONS
are a huge part of the conversation: have an opinion? BRING IT to the discussion –
and read Sheila Farr’s Seattle Times article to get you thinking!
