dorkbot-sea, in collaboration with Seattle-based Net Label Comfort Stand Records, presents a combination CD and online compilation of work by contemporary experimental sound artists, musicians and composers using electricity in new and interesting ways. The simultaneous release of the physical and online versions of the compilation, called People Doing Strange Things With Electricity Too, coincides with the opening of dorkbot-sea’s People Doing Strange Things With Electricity II exhibition at Seattle’s Center on Contemporary Art (CoCA) on January 22nd, 2005. The physical CD will be distributed to exhibition attendees, reviewers, press and exhibiting artists; the online release is freely available for download from Comfort Stand Records. All works included in the release will be published under a Creative Commons Attribution Non-Commercial Share Alike License, and thus may be freely re-sampled and re-used for non-commercial projects.
