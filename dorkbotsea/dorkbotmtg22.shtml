Meeting 22

DORKTOBERFEST!

Wednesday, October 5th, 2005
at 7.30 pm

The Elysian
1221 E Pike St
Capitol Hill, Seattle
(corner of Pike and 13th)

ADMISSION IS FREE
RESTAURANT ALL AGES

Here’s how GoogleMaps
tells you to find The Elysian

See you on Wednesday!


This month’s meeting is all about getting to know each other; putting NAMES to DORKBOT FACES, talking about current and future projects, suggesting future presentations and describing what you’d like to see at dorkbot meetings during our third year.

It’s a purely social evening, with no formal presentations, in an informal setting: a local award-winning brewpub that does GREAT BEER, good food, tea, coffee and cocktails – and provides a spacious venue with tables and seating for allcomers, especially if you RSVP.

That said, please DO bring any portable show and tell items you might have – including your BEERBOTS, things you’re preparing for ROBOTHON, and your images of this weekend’s kinetic sculpture race!
