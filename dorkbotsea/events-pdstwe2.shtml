The second annual dorkbot-sea exhibit of works by local and national artists that use electricity as a significant component of their creation and/or display.

Center on Contemporary Art (CoCA)

JANUARY 22nd 2005 to MARCH 3rd 2005

OPENING PARTY JANUARY 22nd

Accompanying the exhibition will be a SCREENING OF RARE AND UNIQUE 1960’s ELECTRIC ARTS curated by media arts historian ROBIN OPPENHEIMER.


The 2005 dorkbotsea PEOPLES’ CHOICE AWARD of $1,000 was won by San Francisco artist RYAN WOLFE for ‘Sketch of a Field of Grass, Pacific Coast, 2004’. See media alert here.
