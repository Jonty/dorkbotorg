ELIZABETH CONNER

Elizabeth Conner is fascinated by the natural and working histories of public places. She has 15 years of experience in the field of environmental art, collaborating with architects, engineers, landscape architects, urban planners, geologists, and transportation planners. Her work is site-specific and collaborative; materials and methods vary. Projects include a police precinct, a stormwater treatment facility, a library, and a Superfund cleanup site.
