JESSE ALLISON

Jesse Allison is a composer and interactive sonic artist who has focused his graduate research on interactive computer interfaces at the University of Missouri – Kansas City. His work on sensor interfaces has been presented at numerous computer music conferences across the United States and Canada. Exhibitions of interactive sound installations using his sensor interface technology have been programmed in museums across the US and in Sweden. During his tenure at UMKC he has held numerous leadership positions in both departmental organizations and faculty development programs.
