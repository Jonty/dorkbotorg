GINNY RUFFNER

ARTIST: Ginny Ruffner
TITLE: How Y Became RGB
MATERIALS: Metal, glass, video monitors, video camera, mirror
DIMENSIONS: 80" diameter, 34" height
DATE: 2005

DESCRIPTION
Metal-framed chandelier incorporating lamp-worked and blown glass, video monitors and a video camera. The images shown on the video monitors are from the input of the video camera, which is aimed at the mirror to create video feedback. The video monitors provide the main light source for the chandelier.

STATEMENT
Ginny Ruffner's work is exhibited nationally and internationally, and is represented in museum, public and private collections worldwide. She is represented in Seattle by Woodside Braseth Gallery.
