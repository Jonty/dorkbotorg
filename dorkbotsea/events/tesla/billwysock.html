BILL WYSOCK

30 YEARS OF ARCS AND SPARKS… Bill Wysock has been building Tesla Coils since 1962, and founded Tesla Technology Research in January, 1975. The Los Angeles, California-based company has been in continuous operation ever since. This is a unique chance to hear Bill describe his work, and to see slides and video of some of his favourite projects - from "Fear Factor Live" to the Singapore Science Center, from Hollywood films to museums and exhibits across the world.

More about Bill…
Full bio and company profile at http://www.ttr.com/Company_profile.htm. Recent projects include a large installation for the Science Center of Iowa (SCI), Universal Studios "Fear Factor Live", Mid America Science Musem (MASM) in Hot Springs, Arkansas and three major displays for the Singapore Science Center (SSC).
