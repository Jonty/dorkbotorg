BIO
Syphilis Sauna is the Solo Project of Patrick Urn and is intended to be the more experimental side of electronic music. Using a variety of homemade/store bought devices and very little "structure" Syphilis Sauna builds and eclectic array of sounds and and textures. Another main focus is random collaboration and music being a cure for the "human disease".
