JOSHUA HERRALA

ARTIST: Joshua Herrala
TITLE: Facade
LENGTH: 6:36

DESCRIPTION
Ambient/Illbient track produced using both traditional synthesis and processed 'found sounds'. The goal was to create a space, and let the music disappear.

STATEMENT
Josh has been involved with techno since the 'third wave' in the early 90's. Like many in the Detroit area, he was heavily influenced by the radio host The Electrifying Mojo during the 1980s.

His music still maintains ties to the warmth of the analog past. Typically lacking in many breaks or stabs, it ebbs and flows subtly. He focuses on making music for the mind as well as body.

Josh Herrala runs Deluxe Curl Records.
