PATRICK O’DOWD AND CHRISTOPHER O’DOWD

TITLE: Solar Music
MATERIALS: Wood, metal, glass and silicon
DIMENSIONS: 4" x 5" x 3"
DATE: 2003

TITLE: Snakebot v 1.0
DIMENSIONS: 13" x 3"
DATE: 2003

DESCRIPTION
Solar Music: the power of photons effecting sight and hearing. This piece was conceived with the idea that the music it made should be a familiar tune but presented in a way that was unfamiliar. The work should also be small enough to be located in an unassuming and inconspicuous location, so when an individual heard the music it might incline them to stop and listen for a moment or ignore it and move on as they choose.
