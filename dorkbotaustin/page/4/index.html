Dorkbot 21 – @Cafe Mundi

Aug 31st

Posted by npietran in Events

Thurs. Oct 15th 2009 7:30-9:00pm

Cafe Mundi 1704 E 5th St. (a few blocks east of I-35) (512) 236-8634 FREE EVENT

Still in the planning stages, but we’ve set the date!

If you have a project or something you’d like to present, let us know!

Current Presenters:

  • Alan Watts presents his new animation app Toon Fu
  • Mikal Hart shows off his GPS Box
  • Andre Lamothe demos the new chameleons
  • Dr. Bleep, Dann Green, and Eric Archer annouce the Handmade Music Austin workshop series, and demo their new Andromeda Space Rockers mini-instruments
  • John Boiles shows off his ipod contolled devices (1, 2)
  • Brooks Coleman
  • Marvin Niebuhr
Dorkbot-austin is a regular meeting of artists (sound / image / movement / whatever), hackers, designers, engineers, students and other interested parties who are involved in the creation of electronic art (in the broadest  sense of the term).

Dorkbot is, as always, a FREE event.

Dorkbot Austin – New volunteer

Aug 29th

Posted by npietran in Blog

Dorkbot Austin would like to welcome a new volunteer to the group — Laura Smith.

Laura is a web designer in the Faculty Innovation Center at UT’s Cockrell School of Engineering. She’ll be helping to coordinate our upcoming events, so feel free to contact her if you’d like to speak at our next event or to help out in some way. dorkbotaustin [@] dorkbot [.] org.

If you are interested in volunteering for Dorkbot Austin, please contact us. We can always use extra folks for setup or teardown at our events. Or if ‘you know a guy who knows a guy who has a friend’ who might like to present at our next event, just let us know. dorkbotaustin [@] dorkbot [.] org.
