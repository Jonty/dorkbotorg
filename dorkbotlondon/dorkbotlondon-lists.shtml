dorkbotlondon-blabber

dorkbotlondon-blabber mailing list is a forum for electonic artists and other interested parties to discuss issues related to making and presenting their work in the london area. it's an extension of the dorkbotlondon meetings, but even if you can't come to the meetings in person you're still welcome to participate on the list.

example topics: where do i get a flame thrower in london? what performance space will let me release pigeons into the audience? is argos the cheapest place to get long plastic dowels? which maplins in london doesn't suck and where can i get capacitors at 3am? how do you get a permit to perform on the N253 northbound and is there an AC outlet i can use on speaker's corner?

please don't send show announcements/listings/etc to the list, unless they're extra-special one-offs.

to subscribe to dorkbotlondon-blabber enter your email address, select regular or digest mode and click the button.

mode:

    majordomo@music.columbia.edu

    majordomo@music.columbia.edu

    dorkbotlondon-blabber@music.columbia.edu
