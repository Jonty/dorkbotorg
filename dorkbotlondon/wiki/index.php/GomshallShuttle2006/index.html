Shuttle Times

The Shuttle will probably not be doing more than one trip every 2-3 hours. Try to aim to arrive at one of the times listed, and then we can organise some kind of transport convoy. These times are approximate, if there is serious need for transport at other times, then make some noise and well put on extra lifts.

Shuttle Payment

The shuttle could probably use some decoration. Fuzzy dice, head nodders etc. Make a donation. If you would be happy to operate as a designated driver and share the transport load, please email saul at theps dot net (before 8am on the 1st)
