featuring the seasonal and organic...

James Larsson, promising to show us a 'game controller for VERY sad men', while Mike 'Dr. Voltage' Harrison, will be inviting us to an interactive, participatory performance of the 'bluetooth-o-phone - bluetoothery at the edge of uselessness'. Dancing into the night is facilitated by plenty of champagne and Slub.

RSVP!

Limehouse DLR from Bank or Tower Gateway

By Bus
15 from Trafalgar Sq / 115 from Aldgate / D6 from Hackney / D3 from Bethnal Green

Get onto Commercial Road and travel East from Aldgate or Whitechapel. Keep going until you pass Limehouse DLR on your right (you go under the DLR train bridge) and you'll see a modern red brick church also on the right, then the library with a statue of Clement Atlee in front of it, then you'll see the Town Hall, with the Limehouse Hawksmoor church behind it. At this point you should get off the bus/out of the car and come ring on the "Boxing Club" bell.
