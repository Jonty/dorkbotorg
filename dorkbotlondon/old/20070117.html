Directions to state51

[map]

appearance – factory surrounded by metal fence and with ‘state51’ in big letters on the gate.

from liverpool street tube – walk north up bishopsgate (which becomes shoreditch high street), then turn right into bethnal green road, left into brick lane and first left into rhoda street.

from the number 8 bus – it goes along bethnal green road – get off at the brick lane/bethnal green road crossroads and go north up brick lane and first left into rhoda street.

from bethnal green tube – walk west along bethnal green road, about 15 minutes later you should hit the brick lane crossroads – turn right then first left into rhoda street.
