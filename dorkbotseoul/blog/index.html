about Dorkbot Seoul

Thursday 28 December 2006 at 03:54 am

Hello,
Dorkbot Seoul is a group that is interested in using electronics and new media to make art bots or DIY projects in Seoul, Korea.
The group is pretty small at this point, however you are welcome to join our mailing list and we would like to have your opinion, thoughts or contributions.
The announce and blabber lists are set up, so you can subscribe at:

announce mailing list
blabber mailing list

Hopefully we can see you in our mailing list! Thanks!
