dorkbotsf mailing lists:

The dorkbotsf-blabber list is meant for electronic/machine artists and other interested parties to discuss issues related to making and presenting their work in the Bay Area. It's an extension of the dorkbotsf meetings, but even if you can't come to the meetings in person you're still welcome to participate on the list
