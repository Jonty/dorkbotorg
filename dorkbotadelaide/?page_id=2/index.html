About

Dorkbot-Adelaide is part of a new worldwide movement of artists, engineers, designers, hackers who meet to explore and discuss strange or new ways to use electricity and technology.

Meetings are free event, occur every two month and are held at the Electronic Music Unit, University of Adelaide (for more information about where and how to get there please visit the location page).

If you want to stay in contact subscribe to the Dorkbot Adelaide Blabber List.
