MCG: dorkbot303 would like to thank the Media Creation Group MCG for hosting dorkbot303 v6.01 celebrating our 6th year of dorkbot events in Colorado.

BINDERY|space: dorkbot303 would like to thank the BINDERY|space for hosting dorkbot303 v5.02. It was a great opportunity for some of our larger installation artists to realize their entire electronic art pieces. The space is awesome and we appreciate their invitation to host our unique events.

dorkbot @ DOM: dorkbot303 would like to thank Denver Open Media for their continued support of the dorkbot annual events at their studio. This has given many of our dorkbot presenters the unique opportunity to have their performances and presentations live broadcasted on the DOM public access station Cabel Channel 57 Denver.

Burning dorks: dorkbot303 would like to thank Boulder Burn Works for hosting a special series dedicated to the Electronic Artists of Burning Man. We featured local Burning Dorks and their radically inclusive, moop free, glowing, fire like projects, The Glow Fur drum kit was a super hit. Plus a Happy Thursday E-Cruiser Bike Show that rocked Boulder local bike scene.
