2/ Mikael Enqvist & Jörgen Andersson

Dorkbot-GBG #2 took place at Röda Sten on Friday 19th September 2008, followed by a club event organized by Dånk! Collective.



Schedule
Dorkbot: 17.00 - 20.00 (first presentation at 18.00)
Dånk! night: 20.00 - 21.00 (Free entrance before 20.00)

Presenters
Jörgen Andersson (Exhadley): circuit bending, its history and basics [slides] [video].
Mikael Enqvist (Pistol Disco): homemade fan-organ [video].
During the afternoon people from Minimal Effort constructed a geodesic dome outside of the Röda Sten building, as a possible arena for electronic art and music [video]. One of the members gave a brief presentation about their work and about the history and future of the geodesic domes.

Klubb Dånk!
The club event that followed Dorkbot included DJs, VJs and live performances by electronic music artists. More information here. Press release (in Swedish) here.

    Dorkbot is a group of affiliated organizations worldwide that sponsor grassroots meetings of artists, engineers, designers, scientists, inventors, and anyone else who loves to build things, hack things, play with things, do whatever strange things you can do with electricity. Dorkbot-GBG is a dorkbot that takes place in Göteborg, Sweden, since May 2008.
