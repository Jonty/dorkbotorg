Göteborg’s very first Dorkbot event took place on 2nd May 2008. We had invited Japanese artist Kanta Horio to talk about his work. As he is one of the organisers of Dorkbot-Tokyo, he also had many fascinating projects from Japan to show us. The local swedish radio station SR P4 Göteborg was present, and interviewed Kanta and a couple of Dorkbot-GBG organisers live and direct.

    Dorkbot is a group of affiliated organizations worldwide that sponsor grassroots meetings of artists, engineers, designers, scientists, inventors, and anyone else who loves to build things, hack things, play with things, do whatever strange things you can do with electricity. Dorkbot-GBG is a dorkbot that takes place in Göteborg, Sweden, since May 2008.
