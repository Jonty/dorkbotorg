4/ Fredric Bergström & Alex Berman

Dorkbot-GBG #4 took place on 24th October at Röda Sten in Göteborg, in conjunction with United Modulations Day. Fredric Bergström and Alex Berman (a.k.a Nim) presented their custom-built musical interfaces and both performed later on the same night.



Time: 19.00
Location: Röda Sten
Fee: Free entrance for dorkbot, fee for the rest of the evening

Presenters
Fredric Bergström is a sound artist, musician and member of several bands such as the death jazz / noise group Läder. At Dorkbot he showed his hurdy-gurdy (bowed stringed musical instrument, vevlira in Swedish) processed by iphone and computer.

Alex Berman a.k.a Nim performs live with cassette walkmans serving as musical instruments and simultanously as video players for found super-8 film. By manipulating the walkmans mechanically and processing the signal with guitar effects, rhythm and distorsion in video and audio is achieved.

4/ Fredric Bergström & Alex Berman

    Dorkbot is a group of affiliated organizations worldwide that sponsor grassroots meetings of artists, engineers, designers, scientists, inventors, and anyone else who loves to build things, hack things, play with things, do whatever strange things you can do with electricity.
