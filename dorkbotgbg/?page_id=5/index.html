About Dorkbot-GBG

Dorkbot-GBG is a series of dorkbot events organised in Göteborg, Sweden, by Dånk! Collective. Just like in all the other dorkbots around the globe, the purpose of these meetings is to nurture the local electronic arts and interaction design community. They are meant to allow both emerging and established artists/designers/hackers/scientists/bricoleurs - local or international - to present new works and freely exchange ideas about arts and design of electric nature. Certain events might also include performances and workshops.

All events are free of charge (except for potential material costs when taking part in a hands-on workshop) and open to anyone who is interested in the topic.
Dorkbot-GBG events take place regularly but at different frequencies and locations. This webpage gathers information about past and future events.

    Dorkbot is a group of affiliated organizations worldwide that sponsor grassroots meetings of artists, engineers, designers, scientists, inventors, and anyone else who loves to build things, hack things, play with things, do whatever strange things you can do with electricity. Dorkbot-GBG is a dorkbot that takes place in Göteborg, Sweden, since May 2008.
