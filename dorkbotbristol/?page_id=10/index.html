Mailing List

Keep up to date with events, news and other stuff by subscribing to the dorkbot-bristol mailing list.  This is a receive only list where we just let you know about upcoming stuff.

If you’re interested in getting more involved in the process of planning and organising Dorkbot Bristol events and gatherings, then the dorkbot-bristol blabber mailing list is the list for you.  Click the link to sign up and then send us an e-mail to let us know your ideas.

Please note that the dorkbot global mailing lists are experiencing an outage at the moment, please email us at dorkbotbristol [at] googlemail [dot] com instead.  Thanks.

You may also drop us a line at dorkbotbristol [at] googlemail [dot] com if you would like to host a future event, have a programme idea of would like to get involved in future dorkbotic activities in the West of England.
