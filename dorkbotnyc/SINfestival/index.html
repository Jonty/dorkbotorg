people doing strange things with electricity

some dorkbots set up visuals and installations for the SIN Festival after-party at LUX in williamsburg on sunday, august 25th. present were:

  • Andrea Urist: Musical Devices
    weird musical sculpture

    http://fargo.itp.tsoa.nyu.edu/~urist/musicgeek

  • Luke DuBois & Mark McNamara: Soundscape Navigator
    cool interactive video/sound app

    http://www.music.columbia.edu/~luke/ssn/

  • isami ching & Alex Lee: Untitled
    giant throbbing inflatable pillow

    http://music.columbia.edu/~nicholas/soundmov/index.html

  • douglas repetto: How to Annoy a Plant
    bio-art + video installation

    http://music.columbia.edu/~douglas

  • linda lulu rath: videography
we all had a good, if long and smoky and loud, time! special thanks to andrea, luke, mark, isami, me, and lulu for helping out.
