people doing strange things with electricity

we celebrated dorkbot-nyc's two year birthday on 04.dec.2002, with a gear-stuffed meeting that featured the lovely and talented:

Eric Singer: presented LEMUR's musical robots. LEMUR (League of Electronic Musical Urban Robots) is a group of musicians, artists, technologists and roboticists who have been creating an orchestra of robotic musical instruments. LEMUR is a project of Harvestworks and the Madagascar Institute, funded by a grant from the Rockefeller Foundation.
http://ericsinger.com
http://synesthesia.com (david branciardi)
http://madagascarinstitute.com

Maya Gorton: presented Production Designer, a new multimedia tool that allows you to arrange video, audio, MIDI and serial information on a timeline and control a variety of outputs and hardware devices. Free demos of the software will be available and you will be given an opportunity to participate in a contest to make something with it.
http://wetelectrics.com

Zeljko Blace: showed some work and talked about the south-eastern European media art scene. Zeljko was visiting New York from Croatia.
http://www.mi2.hr
http://www.egoboobits.net
