people doing strange things with electricity

the may 2002 dorkbot-nyc meeting was joint meeting of the New York Chapter of the Robotics Society of America (RSA-NY) and dorkbot. it was held at the NYU ITP conference room.

the presenters were:

  • David Schwarz: Cello, a prototype for an interactive electronic musical instrument

  • Paul Bartlett and colleagues: a talk about their work at Honeybee robotics.

  • NYU ITP Students: current work in robotics and design
