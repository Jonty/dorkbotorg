people doing strange things with electricity

the two hundred eleven thousandth dorkbot-nyc meeting was held on 08.jan.2003 at the Columbia University Computer Music Center.

it featured the lovely and talented:

Cory Arcangel presented recent work revolving around the 6502 microprocessor. Subjects most likely will include acid house, flavor flav, & late 80's commodore 64 video graffiti.

Cory Arcangel is an artist living in manhattan. He is part of the beige programming crew aka BEIGE, and the Radical Software Group.

http://beigerecords.com/cory
http://beigerecords.com

Dafna Naphtali talked about her Max/MSP programs for sound processing of voice and other instruments that she's been creating and using since '93 -- among them an elaborate and sprawling software control patch for the Eventide H3000 effects processor for her {kaleid-o-phone} projects -- and her work at Engine 27, and What is it Like to be a Bat?

http://www.whatbat.org
http://www.engine27.org

Liubo Borissov presented recent work exploring video synthesis from sound and talk about some of the tools available for representing and reinterpreting sound as visual data.

Liubo is a graduate student in physics at Columbia University.

http://www.dadastream.com
