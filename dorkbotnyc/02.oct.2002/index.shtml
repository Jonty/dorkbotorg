another happy dorkbot-nyc meeting was held on 02.oct.2002 at the Columbia University Computer Music Center.

it featured the lovely and talented:

Joshua Goldberg:
dervish, a somewhat idiosyncratic free-as-in-beer video mixing and distortion application that is optimized for surprisingly organic feedback transformations. Information on dervish can be found here: http://www.goldbergs.com/dervish

Pall Thayer:
Intercontinental spontaneous jam session, a web-based multi-user musical interface for the manipulation of abstract imagery. (Live via videophone from Reykjavik, Iceland!)
http://www.this.is/pallit/isjs

Philip Galanter:
COMPLEXITY: Art and Complex Systems, a show currently mounted at the Samuel Dorsky Museum of Art at SUNY New Paltz. Galanter is co-curator of the show, with Ellen K. Levy. http://philipgalanter.com
