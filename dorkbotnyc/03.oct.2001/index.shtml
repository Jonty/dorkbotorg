the nineth dorkbotnyc meeting was held on october 3rd, 2001 at the Columbia University Computer Music Center.

the presenters were:

  • Chris Bailey: A database-based paradigm for mixing concrete sounds

    http://music.columbia.edu/~chris

  • ChanSchatz (Eric Chan & Heather Schatz): Current video work

    hss15@columbia.edu

  • Michael Gogins: CsoundVST, a Windows DLL that contains all of canonical (Bath or John ffitch) Csound in the form of a VST instrument. The project is in a functional beta status.

    http://www.pipeline.com/~gogins
