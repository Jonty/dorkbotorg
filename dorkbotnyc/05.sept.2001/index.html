people doing strange things with electricity

the eigth dorkbotnyc meeting was held on september 5th, 2001 at the Columbia University Computer Music Center.

the presenters were:

  • David Birchfield: realtime genetic algorithm-based approach to generating music.

    http://music.columbia.edu/~dab

  • Elaine Thomazi Freitas & Christina Towle: REFLECT, an interactive music/dance work

    elaine@music.columbia.edu

    there will be a performance of REFLECT at the Dance Now Festival next week.

  • Jason Freeman: TELEPHONE ETUDE #1: SHAKESPEARE CUISINART

    http://music.columbia.edu/~jason
