  • Eric Rosenzveig and Willy Le Maitre: The Appearance Machine

    http://www.appearancemachine.com
    junkmail@w----e.net

  • douglas irving repetto: bleep-ping and other torments of the physical world

  • Marcus Alessi Bittencourt: microtonality, weird tunings, irrational rhythmic cycles, spatialization, and the joys of object oriented programming

    http://music.columbia.edu/~alessi
