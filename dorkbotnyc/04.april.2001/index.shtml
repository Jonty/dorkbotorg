the fifth dorkbotnyc meeting was held on april 4th, 2001.

the presenters were:

  • Dan Trueman: Bowed-Sensor-Speaker-Array
    dan demonstrated and performed his Bowed-Sensor-Speaker-Array, an experimental instrument that combines a variety of sensor instruments inspired by the violin (sensor fingerboard, bow, and bridge) with a multi-channel spherical speaker array.

    http://music.columbia.edu/~dan
    http://trollstilt.org

  • Mark McNamara: tibet project/proposal toward the 360 degree interactive theater
    mark presented a project he's working on in conjunction with the exiled government of tibet as well as his proposal for a 360 virtual stage environment.

    ozark@music.columbia.edu

  • Andrea Urist: Scary Squeezable Blobs:
    Andrea Urist presented some of her wacky homemade electronic instruments.

    http://andrea_urist.home.mindspring.com/musicgeek
    andreaurist@yahoo.com
