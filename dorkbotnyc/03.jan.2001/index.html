people doing strange things with electricity

the second dorkbotnyc meeting was held on january 3rd, 2001. presenters were:

  • kurt ralske: on using nato.0+55 for real-time interactive control of video (including audio-to-video and video-to-audio mapping)
    http://www.miau-miau.com

  • sheldon drake: showed a short reel of his latest adobe after effects creations
    http://www.bway.net/~schnoidl
    schnoidl@bway.net
    bmaffitt@aol.com

  • luke dubois: presented an envirnonment for live video processing he's currently developing
    luke@music.columbia.edu

  • jeremy bernstein: showed some recent video work he's been developing using MAX and NATO.0+55. he's attempting to fit narrative/discursive content into the context of live image performance.
    http://www.bootsquad.com
    www.bootsquad.com/nato/nato.html
    jeremy@bootsquad.com
