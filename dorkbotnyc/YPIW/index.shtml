You're Playing It Wrong:

Creative Musical Misuse

Please join us at 92YTribeca for a selection of historical and contemporary short videos and live performances featuring inappropriate, inadvisable and occasionally disastrous creative musical mayhem.

When: Thursday, March 11, 2010, 8:00pm
Where: 92YTribeca Screening Room, 200 Hudson Street
$$$: $10.00 (SOLD OUT!)
