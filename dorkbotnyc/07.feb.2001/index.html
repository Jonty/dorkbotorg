people doing strange things with electricity

the third dorkbotnyc meeting was held on february 7th, 2001. presenters were:

  • brett mensh: gridly maytricks of bright beacons at midnight
    (video feedback using a digital video camera and various optical devices)

    brett@mensh.com
    http://www.mensh.com

    http://www.videofeedback.dk (contributed by an attendee)

  • stefan prosky and jay judge: 555 Aesthetic s w/ Sound Toys & Installations

    tefman@hotmail.com
    jjudge@aol.com

  • Lukasz Lysakowski: Audio/video synthesis through Imag/ine

    lukasz@wildlifeanalysis.org
    http://www.wildlifeanalysis.org

  • Conrad Parker: boids (java-based boids algorithm) and a brief talk about free software

    http://www.vergenet.net/~conrad

    http://www.ericsinger.com/HTML/Work.html#objects
    (boids algorithm implemented as a match patch; contributed by an attendee)
