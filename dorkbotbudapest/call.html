Dorkbot-Budapest is calling for presentations

The purpose of Dorkbot meetings is to nurture local electronic arts communities and to encourage both emerging, and established artists to present new works for informal peer review; establish a forum for the presentation of new technology related art; help establish relationships and foster collaboration between people with various backgrounds and interests. Dorkbot groups encourage the free exchange of ideas in the field of electronic arts all around the world with at over thirty local Dorkbots holding monthly meetings.

We are looking for people who want to present their art and technology-related projects or ideas (in English or Hungarian) at a Dorkbot-Budapest event.

Some imaginary presentation topics are: a demo of the new motion tracking software you're writing; playing a video you made; an explanation of how you hacked your game boy to get it to make cool bleeping noises; trouble shooting your remote-controlled sandwich making robot; bringing in some dancers you're working with and having them demonstrate the interface used in your new mind-control ballet; discussing your approach to electronic sculpture.

If you have questions or would like to present your project at the next Dorkbot-Budapest contact us at: dorkbotbudapest at dorkbot org.

If you want to stay updated subscribe to our mailing list at:
