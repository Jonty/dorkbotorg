Who is dorkbot Eindhoven? Dorkbot Eindhoven is an initiative of Art & Technology Festival STRP in collaboration with Studium Generale (TU/e).

What is dorkbot Eindhoven? Around the globe there is a movement of small, autonomous functioning organizations with a similar goal: Offering a platform to (mostly local and regional) artists, designers, scientists, nerds, students and others whom, in their work let themselves be inspired by technology. This movement is called Dorkbot. DorkbotEindhoven begins April 6th.

The purpose of dorkbot is: to give artists/programmers/engineers an opportunity for informal peer review; establish a forum for the presentation of new art works/technology/software/hardware; help establish relationships and foster collaboration between people with various backgrounds and interests.

Dorkbot Eindhoven is supported by Stichting City Dynamiek Eindhoven & Gemeente Eindhoven.
