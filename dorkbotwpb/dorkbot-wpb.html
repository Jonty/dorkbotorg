dorkbot.org
“people doing strange things with electricity” (in west palm beach)
 
 
  1. ‣what? dorkbot-wpb is a quarterly informal meeting where interesting people doing strange things can meet strange people doing interesting things and so on, exchanging information, ideas, and viewpoints.  the first dorkbot was in nyc, and now they’re popping up like mushrooms all over the world.
  2. ‣who?  dorkbot-wpb is open to people who are interested in the convergence of technology and creativity, including (but not limited to) artists, programmers, musicians, graphic artists, choreographers, engineers, lighting and set designers, av designers, djs, vjs, live coders, and so on (see the meetings page for more information)
  3. ‣why?  dorkbot-wpb is a place where you can show your work for informal peer-review.  we welcome presentations by fellow dorkbotters of new artistic endeavors, software, hardware, etc.
  4. ‣where? dorkbot-wpb meets at the vera lea rinker hall, room 329 at the campus of palm beach atlantic university in downtown wpb. the vlr building, which houses the school of music and fine arts, is on acacia street between s. dixie and s. olive, four blocks south of okeechobee blvd, and one block north of the norton museum.
  5. (get a map)
  6. ‣how?  subscribe to the announcement list to get meeting information in your email.  for more information or to send me stuff, email dorkbotwpb@dorkbot.org
