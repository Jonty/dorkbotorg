subscribe to dorkbotsocal-announce.

the dorkbotsocal-announce mailing list is used only for announcements related to dorkbot-socal meetings. in other words, only one email will be sent on this list a month, and will give details on when and where the next meeting is, along with a description of what is going on.

subscribe to dorkbotsocal-blabber.

dorkbotsocal-blabber mailing list is a forum for electonic artists and other interested parties to discuss issues related to making and presenting their work in the southern california area. it's an extension of the dorkbotsocal meetings, but even if you can't come to the meetings in person you're still welcome to participate on the list.

example topics: where do i get a flame thrower in irvine? what performance space will let me release bats into the audience? where is a good place in anaheim to get aluminum? which radio shack in san diego doesn't suck and where can i get capacitors at 3am? how do you get a permit to perform on the freeway and is there an AC outlet i can use at the 405 and 55 interchange?
