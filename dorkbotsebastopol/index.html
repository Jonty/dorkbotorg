    Time: 7:00pm Wednesday February 6th, 2008 (come at 6:00 for possible soup or pizza, and early chatting)
    Place: Rich Gibson's house: 241 Florence Ave, Sebastopol, CA 95472
    Events: This was a great meeting! Steve showed off his kinetic sculpture. Brad showed a cool project he did with his ShopBot and did a little intro on motors and demonstrated stepper motor control, Steamboat Ed showed the work he has on a flame callilope, and we talked about making a machine to paint bodies, and alternate power generation.

    Ed is convertign geo metro engines into steam engines, and once you have a steam engine you _almost_ have a steam turbine. And if you can control a device to paint boobies you can control a heliostat solar concentrator to drive that steam turbine.

    Stop thinking about the boobies, focus on the steam turbine from step 1.
