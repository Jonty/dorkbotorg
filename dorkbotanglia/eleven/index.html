dorkbot anglia eleven

Thursday 9th May 2013, 6 - 9pm (Screening at 6pm, talks start at 7pm)
Think Tank, The Pacitti Company, Ipswich Art School Gallery, 1 Upper High Street, Ipswich IP1 3QH

dorkbot anglia is a regular evening of talks and conversations with "people doing strange things with electricity" in the East Anglian area. We are delighted to announce our eleventh meeting as the guests of Pacitti Company, Ipswich on Thursday 9th May. Entry is free.

The evening will start at 6pm with a screening and then at 7pm David Chatting of the Curiosity Collective and Julia Devonshire of the Pacitti Company introduce:

  • Ed Perkins, who has created a live composition system for instruments and electronics based on a real time social, economic and political model.
  • Mark Langford aka Kiteman, who has recently returned from a trip the Large Hadron Collider at CERN.
  • Nat Bocking, who found a lost family album lying in the street in Venice California in around 1992. He will tell us how he traced the people and tells their stories.

Plus, "OpenDorks" (short ad-hoc slots for anyone to talk about their work)

Find out what happened at dorkbot anglia one (July 2010), two (September 2010), three (November 2010), four (February 2011), five (April 2011), six (June 2011), seven (November 2011), eight (March 2012), nine (June 2012) and ten (February 2013).

dorkbot encourages grassroots meetings of artists, engineers, designers, scientists, inventors and anyone else working under the very broad umbrella of electronic art. Started by Douglas Repetto at Columbia University in 2000, dorkbot is now global in almost 100 cities; dorkbot anglia is one of the newest.

dorkbot anglia to runs every two or three months in a different town in the region. If you would like to participate or could offer a venue, please get in touch -
