                             a CARTE event
                          Passionate Machines
               the art and science of emotional computing

  A  pan-disciplinary conference that brings together key international
 figures from the fields of computing science, psychology, communication
                          theory and fine art

                       Saturday 1st February 2003

 Speakers: Janet Murray
"Love and Ludicism: The Emotional Power of Electronic Games"

Dylan Evans
"Scientific interest in the emotions: a renaissance"

Kenneth Rinaldo
"Laughing and weeping machines"

Sarah Kember
"Narratives of Emotion"

Steve Grand with Lucy the robot baby orangutan
"Machines like us"

Chairs: Jane Prophet & Dylan Evans

Passionate Machines is organised by the Centre for Arts Research
Technology and Education (CARTE), University of Westminster

Saturday 1st February 20039.00am - 5.30pm
Venue:Hogg Lecture Theatre,
University of Westminster
35 Marylebone Road,
London NW1
(Nearest Tube: Baker Street)

for further details
www.carte.org.uk/passionatemachines
Telephone 020 7911 5000 Ext 2675
Fax 020 7911 5439
email: sue@carte.org.uk
