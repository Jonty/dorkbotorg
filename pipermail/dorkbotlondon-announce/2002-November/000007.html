Hello,

I know you've all been tearing your hair out worrying that there's been no
dorkbotlondon announced for November, but worry no longer, we've been saving
up all our energy, ideas and money for a huge blowout in December.

I know we've annouched Dorkbotlondon's birthday at the last 2 dorkbots, but

"dorkbotlondon" in big red crayon over the *whole* evening, and probably the
next few days too, just to be safe.

a birthday), and dress code will follow shortly.

Until then, to stave off withdrawl symptoms of various kinds, a
dorkbotlondonsocial will kick off this wednesday (13th November) from 8pm at
the Golden Heart pub opposite Spitalfields E1.

see you there,
