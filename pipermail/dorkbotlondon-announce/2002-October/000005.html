EXTRA 
slow sound system 

The slow club returns: thinking space for relaxed audio experiments. A patchwork of digitalia, audioscapes, collage and ethnographic forgery. 

This month, guest dj field recordings maestro .m u r m e r (framework, resonance fm) plays found sound manipulations. 

Plus EXTRA vs. OrangeAge global soundclash: slow sound system remixed on the fly by nuuk and cohorts, simulcast from Oakland, SF. 

The show will be simulcast on Resonance 104.4FM, from 3-5pm. Set your receivers, or go to www.resonancefm.com for live streaming audio. 

Full details below.
