No way dude. Don't do it!

It's just more hassle than you need when you have to work out why the  
server's not working and if you are running lives sites then ADSL just  
isn't reliable enough. Plus, you don't have the resources to do proper  
24 hour support and stuff. I don't know if these sites are yours or  
ones you've built for clients, but if you come in drunk at midnight on  
a Saturday and get a call saying "why is my site not working?" what are  
you going to do?

Web hosting with a good host is cheap and reliable, your time is much  
more valuable and you should use it for more interesting things IMHO.
