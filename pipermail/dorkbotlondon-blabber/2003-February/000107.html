      time: 7pm, wednesday 19th February 2003

      location: state51 (directions are below)

      entrance: free

      speakers:
        ashraf nehru   - "frankie the robot"
        kevin carter   - "oral tradition"
        michael linton - "openmoney.org"

      plus opendork! featuring lottie child,
      adam hoyle, jeremy ruston and more
      (maybe you?)

      appearance:
      state51 is a factory surrounded by metal
      fence and with 'state51' in big letters
      on the gate.  enter the courtyard and bear
      right.

      directions from liverpool street tube:
      walk north up bishopsgate (which becomes
      shoreditch high street), then turn right
      into bethnal green road, left into brick
      lane and first left into rhoda street.

      from the number 8 and 388 buses:
      they both go along bethnal green road -
      get off at the brick lane/bethnal green
      road crossroads and go north up brick lane
      and first left into rhoda street.

      from bethnal green tube:
      walk west along bethnal green road, about
      15 minutes later you should hit the brick
      lane crossroads - turn right then first
      left into rhoda street.
