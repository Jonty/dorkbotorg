Hi dorkies,

A friend of mine Karen Marcelo (who herds dorkbotsf) is pimpin' a
project she's working on with the Australian artist Stelarc. There's
some stuff happening this week and early next at the ICA:

  Installation is from 7-12 March
  Presentation is at 8:00 pm, Friday 7 March
  Panel Discussion "Prosthetics- Technology and the Human" is at 4:00 pm
  on Sunday 9 March

Karen says:
  ``it's a 3D model of stelarc's head that talks to you when you type a
  question. we used alicebot for the 'brains' and IBM's text to speech
  software to create the voice dynamically and eyematic's proprietary text
  to animation to sync the voice and animate the head.''

Mad.

http://prosthetichead.org/

If anyone goes along she'd be delighted to hear about the reaction from
the audience, how it goes, etc. karen(at)srl.org

Cheers,
Paul (can't make either dates unfortunately)
