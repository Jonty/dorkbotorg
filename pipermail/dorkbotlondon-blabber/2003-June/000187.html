This may be of interest to some DorkBotters, hope to see some of you there
_____________________________________________________

Well, what do you know...it pays to be a Recordist!

S.Higgins and I both received art grants from the Canada Council earlier
this year for Recordist/Surrealist-related work in performance art (go
figure!).

So guess what...we're taking a trip!

We'll be visiting surrealist friends and other fascinating folk in the U.K.
and in Paris between June 26 and July 14.

In addition to the various meetings we've arranged (details of which I'll be
sure to pass along at a later date), we've also arranged to do some
performances in London (mostly improvised music gigs for myself and our
friend from the Stockholm Surrealist Group Johannes Bergmark). Here's a
run-down:


June 30, The Bonnington Centre,11 Vauxhall Grove, London, SW8 on the corner
of Vauxhall Grove and Bonnington Square, Admission £4/£3 Info: 01932 571323
Start: 8pm
- Johannes Bergmark, invented instruments, voice, electronics; Phil Morton,
accidents and treatments; William Davison, invented instruments, amplified
objects
+ A Hint of Papyrus: the official Johannes Bergmark tribute band 
+ starring
Anders Arentoft, Romuald Wadych, Will Fowler and The Bohman Fantasy.

July 1 - The Klinker, held at the Sussex, 107 Culford Road, London, N1,
info: 0208 806 8216, Start: 9pm, £5/£2
- Johannes Bergmark, invented instruments, voice, electronics; Hugh
Metcalfe, guitar and live electronics, violin, hi-hat, bass drum, gas-mask
voice, etc.; Paul Shearsmith, trumpets; William Davison, guitar and invented
instruments
+  Yumi Hara & Reiko Manabe + John Clarke.

July 2 - Free Radicals, held at the Red Rose Club, 129 Seven Sisters Rd,
London, N7, Info: 020 7263 7265, Start: 8:15pm, £5
- Johannes Bergmark, invented instruments, voice, electronics; Tony Wren,
double bass; William Davison, guitar and invented instruments
+ Roland Ramanan Quartet.

July 3 - "The Clear Spot" on Resonance FM, 7pm
- "Three out of Six Heads" - James Bailey, Sherri Lyn Higgins, and William
Davison, three members of the Canadian free-improv surrealist sound collage
group Six Heads will perform and record live (hand-built instruments, toys
and objects, prerecorded material, etc.) and discuss their process, other
projects, etc., while back in Toronto, the other half of Six Heads will
record the usual monthly jam session. The recordings made by both halves of
the group will be further collaged together at a later date. Joining James,
Sherri, and William in the studio will be Robert Atwood, former member of
Urban Refuse Group. (note: the date for this performance may be bumped to
the following week, please check the Resonance FM website for the most
current schedule, http://www.resonancefm.com/ )

July 4 - The Centurion,1 Deptford Broadway, London, SE8 (DLR Deptford
Bridge), Start: 8.30pm, £4/£3
- Johannes Bergmark, invented instruments, voice, electronics; Paul Hood,
record player, samplers, MDs; William Davison, guitar and invented
instruments
+ Tom Scott, David Aylward, Adrian Northover + Rob Mills

Hope to see those of you in the London area at some of these shows! If you
can't make it, please do tune in to Resonance FM (available via internet
too) on Thursday the 3rd (unless the date gets changed as noted above).

- William A. Davison
International Bureau of Recordist Investigation www.recordism.com
