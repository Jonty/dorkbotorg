The full ten episodes of variablemedia's most recent project 'My Voice in
Your Head' by artists Edward Dorrian and Sally Morfill will remain at
variablemedia.org for just one more week.

'My Voice in Your Head' at: http://www.variablemedia.org

Following this project is 'Free Download' a work by Michelle Deignan and
Simon Goodwin. Starting on the 23rd of September, with daily updates at
variablemedia.org, this new work will run for five weeks. Each day one email
address from the variablemedia mailing list will be sent a password
protected link to a personalised image file. Those who subscribe to the list
during the projects run will also be in with a chance to recieve a unique
free gift!

Information about variablemedia projects at: http://www.variablemedia.info
