Don't worry chaps and chapesses,

Dorkbot is, of course, a private event open only to members of the
dorkbotlondon mailing lists and their invited guests ;-). We wouldn't dream
of having public events, let alone 'live music events'. If you look at
article 232(b) of the proposed legislation, you will see that non-human life
forms are specifically exempt from this piece of legislation, so the
generative music that is (plausibly...deniably...intentionally) performed at
dorkbot is quite safe from punitive fines.

you can't fine a piece of software can you?
