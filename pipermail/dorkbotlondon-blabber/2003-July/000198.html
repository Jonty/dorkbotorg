two hot dates for this weekend:

saturday: london headphones festival, state51, 12pm - 12am

The Placard London Headphones Festival happens on july 19 at state 51's impressive warehouse space on rhoda st, near brick lane. the cream of London's underground electronic music scene - and beyond - is brought together for a day and night of headphone performances.

audio and video performances from John Eacott, Jem Finer, Si-cut.db, Chesh, Sumugan Sivanesan, Colin Potter, .m u r m e r, Jonathan Coleclough, Richard Thomas, Anthony Guerra, Casette41/GR., Datamath, We're Breaking Up, Dallas Simpson, Fabrice Mogini, BitTonic, Dual, Max Eastley, CrowdFormation, PowerBooks For Peace, Motion, iMax, Brown Sierra, Peter
Cusack, Filmgruppe Chaos, Holkham, Gary Jeff, Icarus, Voodoo Muzak vs. David Kristian, Ixi Project, Gagarin, Leafcutter John, Tex La Homa, AMP, Ryo-Co, LJ Kruzer, Rashamon, Sick Lincoln, Frankie The Robot, Cursor Miner and Jay Ropinsky.

http://placard.slab.org

sunday: slow sound system afterparty, the foundry, 3pm-8pm

an afternoon of relaxed audio at the foundry bar and gallery, greate eastern st, london n1.
with crucial felix (aka timeblind, orthlong) on sax and supercollider, local heroes slub live, plus the legendary jesse belle (not clickable, big chill) playing wonky melancholia. and of course residents imax and crome yellow on dfx, with sumugan sivanesan and jay ropinsky on video duty.

http://www.slowsound.net
