attention!

as per all previous mails bar the last one, the london headphones festival is happening on july 19, 12pm-12am. NOT july 20 - that's when the afterparty is happening at the foundry (streamed on resonance fm, 3-5pm BST btw). 

sorry for any confusion ... 

full and correct details below. 

-----

placard. headphone concerts for headphone people

presents

-----

placard headphone festival of experimental and electronic music 
nyc, july 17-19
placard london headphones festival 
london, july 19

.... think of a festival in reverse. performances will only be heard via
headphones in the venue, and broadcast to the world via the net. for once,
it's a perfect opportunity for some focused listening. bring your own
headphones or borrow ours: plug in, switch on, tune out.

-----

NYC's Placard Headphone Festival of Experimental and Electronic Music will
take place on july 17-19. over 30 musicians from all over the world will
be performing for three glorious and inexpensive days at the tank, a new
venue for performing and visual arts in hell's kitchen.

performers include andrew hayleck, andrew neumann, anthony lowe, beauty
contest, ben owen / michael farley, Bit Shifter, Claudio Parodi, david
more, Edmund Mooney,  Evidence with LMNOPF, Fall O' the Equinox, grundik,
jacob danziger, James  Livingston and Loren Dempster, John Kannenberg,
kaspar--or n(one), kendra  juul, loadbang, Lukasz Kamil Lysakowski and
David Michael, Matt Mehlan, Mike Duffey, Nicholas D. Kent and John
Blackford, No One Receiving, omnid, Peter Blasser, R. Luke Dubois and
Johnathan F. Lee, shinkoyo collective, The Night, and zeljko mcmullen.

Details: http://www.thetanknyc.com/placard/

The Placard London Headphones Festival happens on july 19 at state 51, an
impressive warehouse space near brick lane. the cream of London's
underground electronic music scene - and beyond - is brought together for
a day and night of headphone performances.

audio and video performances from John Eacott, Jem Finer, Si-cut.db,
Chesh, Sumugan Sivanesan, Colin Potter, .m u r m e r, Jonathan Coleclough,
Richard Thomas, Anthony Guerra, Casette41/GR., Datamath, We're Breaking
Up, Dallas Simpson, Fabrice Mogini, BitTonic, Dual, Max Eastley,
CrowdFormation, PowerBooks For Peace, Motion, iMax, Brown Sierra, Peter
Cusack, Filmgruppe Chaos, Holkham, Gary Jeff, Icarus, Voodoo Muzak vs.
David Kristian, Ixi Project, Gagarin, Leafcutter John, Tex La Homa, AMP,
Ryo-Co, LJ Kruzer, Rashamon, Sick Lincoln, Frankie The Robot, Cursor Miner
and Jay Ropinsky.

Details: http://placard.slab.org
