SLOW SOUND SYSTEM : JULY 03

big thanks to everyone who came along or tuned in to the london headphones festival and the slow sound system afterparty the other weekend. we're very happy to have been part of such a fantastic weekend of sound and vision. 

we'll be back with more of the same later in the year. right now, we're concentrating on building up the website, establishing an online archives of mixes and live performances, working on a whole series of remix projects - and setting up an online image gallery. expect more on all of this over the next few months. 

in the meantime, here's a flavour of what's on our decks right now. 

happy listening!

best, 

slow sound system
