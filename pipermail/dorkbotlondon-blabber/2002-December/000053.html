dorkbot-nyc
people doing strange things with electricity

next meeting:
wednesday, december 4th, 2002 at 7pm at the Columbia University Computer
Music Center (directions and info at: http://dorkbot.org)

-----

bring beer! bring food! it's dorkbot-nyc's two year birthday! maybe
there'll be a cake. probably not. unless you bring one!

-----

featuring the lovely and talented:

Eric Singer: will present LEMUR's musical robots. LEMUR (League of
Electronic Musical Urban Robots) is a group of musicians, artists,
technologists and roboticists who have been creating an orchestra of
robotic musical instruments. LEMUR is a project of Harvestworks and the
Madagascar Institute, funded by a grant from the Rockefeller Foundation.

Maya Gorton: will present Production Designer, a new multimedia tool
that allows you to arrange video, audio, MIDI and serial information on
a timeline and control a variety of outputs and hardware devices. Free
demos of the software will be available and you will be given an
opportunity to participate in a contest to make something with it.

Zeljko Blace: will show some work and talk about the south-eastern
European media art scene. Zelkjo is visiting New York from Croatia.
