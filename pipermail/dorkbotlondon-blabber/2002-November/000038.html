+a call for submissions that may be of interest to dorkbotters+

File sound art 
sound art on audio cd

call for submissions

In conjunction with the Mead gallery, Warwick Arts Centre, File is producing an audio cd of soundwork reflecting facets of urban experience. The project relates to the Pheonix project, regeneration of Coventry's city centre. File are accepting initial submissions on cd. All types of soundwork welcome: spoken word, field recordings, abstract sound, music, disco, kareoke etc... Participants will recieve cd's. The works will be premiered at the Mead Gallery on Jan 17th 03 as part of the Susanna Heron opening. Cds will be distributed nationally. Deadline November 16. 2002
