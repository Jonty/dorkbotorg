Variablemedia is pleased to announce 'Hemi-demi-semi' a new project by the
artist Richard Couzins. Starting today at variablemedia.org, Couzins
presents a unique, short QuickTime movie loop each day for the 30 days of
this project's duration.

In this project, Couzins employs an economy of means using a minimum of
frames in his movies. Objects and maxims animate and loop on the computer
screen, transient collages that are overwritten each day by a new movie
sequence. These works will have a raw diaristic quality, being made near to
release; 'hot off the press'.

Hemi-demi-semi daily at: http://www.variablemedia.org
Information at : http://www.variablemedia.info
