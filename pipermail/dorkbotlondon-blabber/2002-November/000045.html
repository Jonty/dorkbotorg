hold on to your kishkas, its dorkbotlondon's birthday spectacular!

 7pm *Saturday* 7th December, the boxing club, limehouse town hall

people still doing strange things with electricity, but this time, well
classy: the gentle tinkle of champagne flutes, the elegant, candle-lit
surroundings of limehouse town hall, a bleeping mentalist cabaret that's
been plugged into the mains...

entrance free, but  *RSVPRSVPRSVP*

dorkbot is a monthly meeting of people involved in the creation of
electronic art, in the broadest sense of the term.  entrance is free, and
open to all, but this time, due to venue restrictions and limited
availability of seating, candle-lit ambience and waiters, you'll have to
RSVP if you want to come. Please email your name + who else you're bringing
to dorkbot@twenteenthcentury.com if you want to come. First 100 to reply get
in.

international all-star line up... so far.:

- Wilfried Hou Je Bek - http://www.socialfiction.org - Generative
Psychogeography
- Paul Granjon - http://www.zprod.org/ - will be playing the zitare
- Anne Laforet and Christophe Buffet - http://betaville.wkmx.org
- The Marseille Figs - http://www.marseillefigs.org/ (tbc)

+++ opendork (be one!) and more.

FINDING DORKBOTLONDON

the boxing club, limehouse town hall, commercial street
map:
http://www.streetmap.co.uk/streetmap.dll?grid2map?x=536750&y=181250&zoom=1&i
sp=187&ism=500&arrow=y?95,157

By Tube
Limehouse DLR from Bank or Tower Gateway

By Bus
15 from Trafalgar Sq / 115 from Aldgate / D6 from Hackney / D3 from
Bethnal Green

Get onto Commercial Road and travel East from Aldgate or Whitechapel. Keep
going until you pass Limehouse DLR on your right (you go under the DLR
train bridge) and you'll see a modern red brick church also on the right,
then a pair of ESSO stations on either side of the street, then the
library with a statue of Clement Atlee in front of it, then you'll see the
Town Hall, with the Limehouse Hawksmoor church behind it. At this point
you should get off the bus/out of the car and come ring on the "Boxing
Club" bell.
