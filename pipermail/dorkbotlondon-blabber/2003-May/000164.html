<p>Greetings one and all ~
<p>I have a one-person show at The Lab (gallery) in San Francisco entitled
<i>War
Toys</i> opening this week. The show runs from May 6 until May 24, Tuesdays
through Saturdays, 1:00 to 6:00.
<p><b><font size=+1>The opening reception is this Friday, May 9, from 5:00
to 9:00.</font></b>
<br>If you're based in the Bay Area, I hope you can make it. If you're
not based in the Bay Area, then I <i>really</i> hope you can make it.
<p>The Lab is located at 2948 16th Street at Capp, in San Francisco. Venue
information can be found here:
<p><a href="http://www.thelab.org">http://www.thelab.org</a>
<p>The installation consists of three kinetic/sound sculptures, <i>Airliner,
Sub, </i>and <i>The Crow</i>. You can see the original proposal for <i>Airliner,</i>
a bamboo and plywood airplane with a thirty-foot wingspan here:
<p><a href="http://www.infoflow.com/larnie/airliner">http://www.infoflow.com/larnie/airliner</a>
<p>You can see and hear <i>The Crow</i> here:
<p><a href="http://www.infoflow.com/larnie/crow">http://www.infoflow.com/larnie/crow</a>
<p>Also: Targodie (Larnie Fox and Scot Jenerik) will perform at the Lab
Friday, 5/23 at 8:00 pm. You can find out more about Targodie here:
<p><a href="http://www.infoflow.com/larnie/sound">http://www.infoflow.com/larnie/sound.html</a>
<p>Also also: There will be a group dicewalk as a closing event Saturday,
May 24, beginning at the Lab. We will meet at 1:00 pm. You can find out
more about dicewalks here:
<p><a href="http://www.infoflow.com/larnie/dicewalk">http://www.infoflow.com/larnie/dicewalk</a>
<p>Thanks for your attention.
<p>Best wishes ~
